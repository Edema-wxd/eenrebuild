Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does EDGE certification take?
The EDGE certification process typically takes 6-8 weeks from design submission to final certification, depending on project complexity and documentation readiness.
Do you provide installation services?
Yes, we provide complete installation services for all our products including Neoperl water fixtures, solar systems, and smart meters through our certified technicians.
What areas do you service?
We service all of Nigeria with our headquarters in Lagos. For large projects, we can mobilize teams nationwide.
How much can I save with your solutions?
Savings vary by solution: Water solutions save up to 70%, solar systems can reduce electricity costs by 95%, and EDGE certification typically results in 20-30% overall operational savings.
Do you offer financing options?
We work with financial partners to provide flexible payment options for larger projects. Contact us to discuss available financing solutions.
What warranty do you provide?
We offer comprehensive warranties: 5 years on solar installations, 10 years on Neoperl products, and ongoing support for all EDGE certified projects.
