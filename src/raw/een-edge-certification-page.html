What is EDGE?

Excellence in Design for Greater Efficiencies - a green building certification system created by IFC, a member of the World Bank Group.

Green Building Made Simple

EDGE is a measurable way for builders to optimize their designs, leading to more investment-worthy and marketable buildings. By keeping certification fast and inexpensive, EDGE keeps pace with the momentum that developers need to stay at the forefront of the green building trend.

Unlike other certification systems, EDGE has been specifically designed for emerging markets like Nigeria, taking into account local challenges and providing achievable standards based on your national average.

BEEC Compliance Made Easy

Nigeria's Building Energy Efficiency Code compliance through EDGE certification - the smart path to regulatory compliance.

What is BEEC?

The Nigerian Building Energy Efficiency Code is a set of guidelines outlining minimum energy efficiency standards for buildings in Nigeria. Developed by the Federal Ministry of Power, Works and Housing with GIZ assistance, BEEC is moving from voluntary to mandatory compliance.

Why EDGE for BEEC Compliance?

  • No past experience needed - user-friendly interface
  • Free software for all calculations
  • Quick, real-time simulations
  • User error eliminated through guided process
  • Accepted by regulatory authorities

BEEC Compliance Paths

Prescriptive Path

Meet all prescriptive requirements of the building code including specific insulation values, window ratios, and equipment efficiencies.

  • Fixed requirements for all buildings
  • No flexibility in design choices
  • May not be optimal for your project

Performance Path (with EDGE)

Demonstrate compliance through energy modeling showing your building performs better than the baseline.

  • Design flexibility to optimize for your needs
  • EDGE software handles all calculations
  • Achieve compliance + international certification
  • Often more cost-effective solutions
